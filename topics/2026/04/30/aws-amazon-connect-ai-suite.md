# AWSが「What's Next with AWS 2026」でAmazon ConnectをAIエージェント駆動の4製品スイートに刷新
Tags: Cloud

- Top announcements of the What's Next with AWS 2026 (2026-04-28)
  https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/top-announcements-of-the-whats-next-with-aws-2026/
