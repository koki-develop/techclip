# Google CloudがNVIDIA GTC 2026でAIインフラ拡充を発表、分数GPUやVera Rubin対応を計画
Tags: Cloud, AI

- Google Cloud AI infrastructure at NVIDIA GTC 2026 (2026-04-13)
  https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/compute/google-cloud-ai-infrastructure-at-nvidia-gtc-2026
